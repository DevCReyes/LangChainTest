France:
The capital of France is Paris. It is known as the "City of Light" and is located in the north of the country, on the banks of the Seine River.

Germany:
The capital of Germany is Berlin. It is a city of great historical and cultural importance, located in the northeast of the country.

Italy:
The capital of Italy is Rome. It is famous for its history, the Colosseum, and as the seat of the Vatican.

Spain:
The capital of Spain is Madrid. It is located in the center of the Iberian Peninsula and is renowned for its nightlife and museums.

Colombia:
The capital of Colombia is Bogota
